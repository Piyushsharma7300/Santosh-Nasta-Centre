About Our Brand

Santosh Nasta Centre

Founded & Managed by Mr. Santosh Kumar

Since 1986, Santosh Nasta Centre has been a beacon of taste, quality, and community. What started as a modest local endeavor has blossomed into a premium cafe and sweet boutique, loved by families and food critics alike. Our commitment remains unwavering: to serve you hygiene-checked, traditional preparations that feel like home.

Our Mission

To delight our guests daily by serving authentic, mouthwatering breakfast recipes and artisanal sweets created with uncompromised freshness and hygiene.

Our Vision

To scale our tradition of outstanding culinary heritage across the region, building modern cafes while maintaining our core family values.

Core Values

Purity of ingredients, strict sanitation control, premium quality hospitality, and building lifelong relationships based on culinary trust.

Chronology

Our Journey

1986

The Humble Beginning

Founded by Mr. Santosh Kumar, Santosh Nasta Centre began as a humble street snack counter with a passion for serving authentic, high-quality traditional Indian snacks. Specializing in freshly prepared potato samosas, crispy kachoris, and flavorful kadhi bhajiya, the journey started with great hope, dedication, love, and the blessings of the community. Today, we proudly continue that legacy by delivering the same homemade taste, freshness, and warmth to every customer.

2005

Establishment of First Store

With the continued trust and support of our customers, Santosh Nasta Centre expanded into a larger, permanent store and grew its team to provide faster and better service. Despite this growth, our menu and authentic recipes have remained unchanged, preserving the traditional flavors that define our identity.

Present & Beyond

Expanding the Legacy

Now operating as a premium local destination. Planning expansion of our franchise network to serve high-grade snacks globally.
